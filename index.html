Legend of Tiny, Pixelated, 1980's man

Legend of Tiny, Pixelated, 1980's man is an HTML 5 canvas game that has 10 different enemies, including a 3 stage boss, each with their own custom build AI. The player can lose life upon being hit and gain life upon collecting a heart. In addition to the entire game, the custom backtrack was created by Scott as well.

Technologies used:

HTML | CSS | JavaScript | HTML5 Canvas | Bootstrap

yo! favo

yo! favo is a full stack app built on Node. Users are able to query the Taste Dive API and then favorite music, movies, tv shows, and games. They can then order this list and share it with their friends.

Technologies used:

Node.js | Express.js | jQuery | EJS Layouts | AJAX | PostgreSQL | Sequelize | Materialize | Taste Dive API

Ryde

This full stack MERN app brings carpooling into the present tech age, making it easy to share rydes to work, weekend destinations, and more. If you know that you'll be driving somewhere, why not pick up some fellow travellers on the way to share the costs?

Technologies used:

React.js | Redux | JSX | Node.js | Express | bcrypt | Passprt | Session | MongoDb | Mongoose
